Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, has captivated the imagination of scientists and space enthusiasts for centuries. Over the past decades, numerous robotic missions have been sent to explore the Martian surface, uncovering evidence of ancient water and geological processes. Notable missions such as the Mars rovers Spirit, Opportunity, and Curiosity have expanded our understanding of Mars' geology and potential habitability. Looking ahead, ambitious plans for human missions to Mars represent the next frontier in space exploration, with the goal of establishing a sustained human presence on the Red Planet. The exploration of Mars continues to inspire scientific discovery and pave the way for future human exploration beyond Earth.
